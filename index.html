About Me

Who am I?

My name is Justin Boers, I am 19 years old, and I am currently pursuing a Software Developer level 4 program.

Education

I started my schooling journey at the elementary level, attending Laurentiusschool in Delft. From there, I continued on to Delfland, a secondary vocational school located in Delft, where I began to focus on the technical aspect of education. This decision stemmed from my longstanding interest in this field, eventually leading me to enroll in the Software Developer Level 4 program that I am currently pursuing.

Work Experience

I have gained a lot of work experience, ranging from working in the hospitality industry as a cook to being employed in various retail positions. This diversity of experiences has acquainted me with different work environments. My career began with a paper route, but I didn't continue it for long. Afterwards, I started working in supermarkets as a regular employee. Subsequently, I also worked in the hospitality industry and spent some time in the kitchen. During my school internship, I had the opportunity to work at my internship company, which appealed to me due to my preference for this field of work. Unfortunately, during this job, I had to perform various other tasks as well. Then, I got the opportunity to work elsewhere and return to a retail environment as an employee, which I also did.
